# Field Analysis (Bourdieu)

Analyzes social field structure, power relations, capital distribution, and habitus patterns based on Bourdieu's sociology.

## Domain
Sociological field theory and social structure analysis.

## What It Does
Maps field positions and relations, identifies capital types and distributions (economic, social, cultural, symbolic), analyzes power relations between dominant and dominated positions, assesses field autonomy, and interprets habitus-structure relationships. Supports qualitative, quantitative, and mixed methods.

## When to Use
- Analyzing social fields (education, politics, art, etc.)
- Identifying power dynamics and capital distributions
- Understanding how habitus shapes and is shaped by field structures
