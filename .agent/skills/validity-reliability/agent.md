# Validity and Reliability Analysis

Psychometric analysis for research measurement quality: internal consistency, test-retest, inter-rater reliability, construct validity, content validity, and criterion validity.

## Domain
Psychometrics and measurement validation.

## What It Does
Assesses measurement design and data quality, calculates reliability coefficients (Cronbach's alpha, omega), performs factor analysis (EFA/CFA), evaluates convergent and discriminant validity, assesses criterion-related validity, and provides APA-formatted statistical reports with effect sizes and confidence intervals.

## When to Use
- Validating survey or measurement instruments
- Assessing scale reliability (internal consistency, test-retest)
- Confirmatory factor analysis for construct validity
- Scale development and optimization
