# Actor-Network Theory (ANT) Analysis

Performs ANT analysis including actor identification, relationship network construction, translation process tracking, and network dynamics.

## Domain
Science and technology studies, socio-technical network analysis.

## What It Does
Maps heterogeneous actor networks (human and non-human), tracks translation processes (problematization, interessement, enrolment, mobilization), analyzes material-semiotic relations, and evaluates network stability. Follows Latour, Callon, and Law's ANT framework.

## When to Use
- Analyzing socio-technical networks with human and non-human actors
- Studying innovation processes, policy networks, or knowledge construction
- Tracing how networks are built and stabilized
