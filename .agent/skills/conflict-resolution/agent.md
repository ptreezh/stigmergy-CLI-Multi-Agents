# Research Conflict Resolution

Resolves theoretical, methodological, interpretive, and value-based disagreements in academic research.

## Domain
Academic discourse mediation and consensus building.

## What It Does
Identifies conflict types (theoretical, methodological, interpretive, values-based), analyzes stakeholder positions and evidence, designs resolution strategies, and facilitates consensus. Applies structured dialogue and evidence integration techniques.

## When to Use
- Resolving theoretical disagreements in research teams
- Mediating methodological controversies
- Building consensus across different stakeholder perspectives
