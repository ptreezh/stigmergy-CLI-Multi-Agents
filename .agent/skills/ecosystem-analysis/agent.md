# Ecosystem Analysis

Comprehensive ecosystem analysis for identifying keystone species, business models, digital transformation effects, and inter-species relationships.

## Domain
Business and natural ecosystem analysis.

## What It Does
Integrates sub-skills for keystone species identification, business model analysis, digital transformation impact, and industry ecosystem mapping. Analyzes both competitive and cooperative relationships within ecosystems.

## When to Use
- Ecosystem-level strategic analysis
- Identifying keystone actors in a market or biological ecosystem
- Studying digital disruption effects on industry structures
