# Internal Communications

Writes internal company communications following organizational formats: 3P updates, newsletters, FAQs, status reports, and more.

## Domain
Corporate internal communication writing.

## What It Does
Provides templates and guidelines for common internal communications: Progress/Plans/Problems (3P) updates, company newsletters, FAQ responses, status reports, leadership updates, project updates, and incident reports. Follows company-preferred formats defined in the examples/ directory.

## When to Use
- Writing any internal company communications
- Drafting team updates, status reports, or newsletters
- Answering FAQs in company voice
