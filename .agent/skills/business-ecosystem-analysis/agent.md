# Business Ecosystem Analysis

Comprehensive business ecosystem analysis integrating multiple sub-skills for strategic assessment.

## Domain
Business strategy and ecosystem analysis.

## What It Does
Integrates sub-skills for ecosystem data collection, relationship analysis, keystone species identification, and digital transformation impact assessment. Provides end-to-end ecosystem mapping and strategic insights.

## When to Use
- Analyzing a complete business ecosystem
- Identifying key players and relationships in a market
- Assessing digital transformation effects on an ecosystem
