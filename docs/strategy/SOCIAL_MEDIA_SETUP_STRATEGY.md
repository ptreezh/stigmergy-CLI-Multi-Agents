### Twitter 发布计划

**每日推文** (1-3条):
- **技术洞察** (40%): AI技术趋势、Multi-Agent系统、知识生产
- **项目更新** (30%): 功能发布、进展更新、里程碑
- **行业评论** (20%): 行业新闻、技术评论、观点分享
- **社区互动** (10%): 回复讨论、转发优质内容

**示例推文**:
```
🧠 Hot take: The future of AI agents isn't bigger models - it's smarter collaboration.

Multi-Agent Debate systems show that diverse perspectives + structured disagreement = higher quality knowledge than any single agent.

That's why Stigmergy is built on consensus computing, not just generation.

#AI #MultiAgent #KnowledgeProduction
```

- 周一: 技术洞察 + 项目进展
